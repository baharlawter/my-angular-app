Hi, my name is Bahar, and I created this app to provide users with more information and tools about personal finances. I have always been interested in saving money, investing, paying off debt, and paying off a house early—these have been my passion topics. Inspired by following Dave Ramsey’s style, I built this app to help you better understand your finances.

  • A calculator page that calculates your debt-to-income ratio
  • A books page where users can purchase financial books through external links
  • A blogs page where you can learn more about managing your finances
